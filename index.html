About Me

I am a PhD student at the Cornell University Autonomous Systems Lab, working with Prof. Mark Campbell. I obtained my Bachelor's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and Master's degree at the Johns Hopkins University. My research interest lies in robotic perception and estimation, particularly problems related to uncertainty quantification of deep learning in self-driving scenes.

Before joining Cornell University, I had the pleasure of working on a variety of topics including scientific computing, medical robotics, and computer vision. View my professional summary here.
